ABOUT US

Who we are and what we do.

PROJECT TINIG:
Transforming the Involvement
of the Youth in Governance

We are a group of three students with one shared mission: to empower the youth through voters’ education.

And a goal to provide young people with clear, reliable, and easy-to-understand information about voting, civic participation, and the importance of making informed choices.

In a time where many first-time voters feel confused or unsure about the election process, we aim to be a helpful guide. Our goal is to inspire the youth to become active, responsible, and informed members of society—because every voice matters, and every vote counts. Together, we believe that an informed generation can shape a better future.
